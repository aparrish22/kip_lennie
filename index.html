Each Cat's Personality

Kip's Personality
Kip is pretty laid back and chills most of the time. Kip loves spending time with his brother Lennie and often times he will take a nap or rest beside you. And Kip has the cutest, little meow ever! So high-pitched!
Lennie's Personality
Lennie is the bane of our existence. He's cute and all, but he is an absolute annoyance in the morning. Usually he will meow pretty loud to get food whenever he wants. However, Lennie is an absolute monster when he gets our attention in the mornings to satiate his hunger. He will crawl up onto us in bed and meow louder than any other speciman alive! He will rub his body and head against our head... and maybe he will attempt to claw or paw us on the face just to wake us up and get our attention so he can get his food!

Fun fact: He's missing the tip of his tail.
